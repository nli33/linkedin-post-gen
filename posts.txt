What is AI SEO?

It's the question I get asked more than anything right now

Business owners, marketing managers, even other agency founders, rveryone wants to know what it is and whether they need it

Here's the simplest answer I can give after 50,000 hours of SEO across 1,000+ clients

AI SEO is SEO, with a new layer on top 🧠

When someone asks ChatGPT a question, it searches the web using Bing and Google, pulls from pages that already rank well, and summarises what it finds

When someone Googles something and gets an AI Overview (which now happens on 50%~ of searches and takes up 75% of your mobile screen)

Google is pulling from pages that already rank in its own search results

Both systems run on the same foundation:

1. Your website needs to clearly explain what you do, where you do it, and why you're the best at it
2. Other credible websites need to mention and link to you
3. Your content needs to answer the exact questions your customers are asking in self-contained, well-structured sections
4. You need to cover your topic better than anyone else in your space

That's AI SEO, the same foundations that have always worked, applied to how search actually works now

Your customers don't care what it's called, they care about typing their problem into Google or ChatGPT and finding you in the answer, that's it
